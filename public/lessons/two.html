2: More Mantras

Review of the bols Learning how to sing to the first mantra Two new mantras

The next mantra we will learn is a variation of the mantra from Lesson 1. We are adding an additional matra to the first part of the mantra. We added a khe in to the second matra:

ta khe - -
khe ta ge ge

Once you have mastered the first mantra and its variation, move onto the next:

ta khe ti khe
ta ge ge -
