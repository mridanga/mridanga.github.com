bhajani tala

8 beat cycle, same as kaherva

this has an off beat nature to it, used in the medium to fast part of the kirtans

found often in bhajans (hence the name)

ghe ta ghe
ghe dha
ghe ta khe
khe ta

ghe taghe ghe dha
ghe takhe -khe ta


variations (double up the ghes)

ghe ta ghe
ghe dha
gheghe ta khe
khe ta

and

gheghe ta ghe
ghe dha
ghe ta khe
khe ta
