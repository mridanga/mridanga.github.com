Learning Tihais.
A Tihasi is used sparingly to add variety to a kirtan. Played in sets of 3 (typically?). used at end of line, mantra, song. 
called a fill in modern drumming.

new notes
jha: synonym for dha

remember, dha is ge + ta

ta khe ta -
