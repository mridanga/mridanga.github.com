Grandaddy mantra (kaherva)

We will learn the most common one played

dha ge ti kha
na ge dhi na

new bols

kha: khe + ta
dhi: dhe, ge + ti
na: ta

practice!

If you have trouble, practice each half individually then combine them. 

Applied in kirtan with a tihai at the end of every cycle
